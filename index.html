Learning Goals

The goal of this curriculum is to engage students with interactive lessons and projects, and to have them think critically about signal processing, AI, and creative human-AI music collaboration. Throughout this course, students will have open-ended discussions on questions such as:

  1. Gain a deeper understanding of human and machine audition, and the similarities and differences between them.
  2. Understand the signal properties that make music sound the way it does, and create music by modifying these properties.
  3. Interact with AI agents to build creative music generation projects.
